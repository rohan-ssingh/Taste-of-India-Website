Our Story

Who We Are

- Founders, Praneel and Rohan, have a dream to not only spread Indian cuisine throughout the world, but also their delicious traditional grandma’s recipes. As children, Rohan and Praneel grew up on their grandma’s food and they would love to spread her food to everyone to enjoy some vibrant food which the Indian cuisine has to offer.


- Using traditional methods of cooking such as the tandoori oven, there are dishes from all parts of India where you can taste the distinctness of each region. The menu highlights fragrant flavors and the careful thorough process of the cooking rather than making fattening unhealthy dishes.


- If you would like a true authentic experience of all Indian Cuisine has to offer, order from our Menu!
